A Comprehensive List of things you should watch, because I said so.

  • Avatar: The Last Airbender (2008) = a pleasure to watch, deemed one of the best shows for all ages
  • Ratatouille (2007): A movie about following your passions despite the odds along with delicious cuisine.
  • Aggretsuko (2018): A comedy anime about the struggles of adulthood.
  • Mulan (1998): Because the new one is so bad, you deserve to relive the original class film.
  • Zumbo's Just Desserts (2016): Baking and ultimate dessert competition on Netflix.
  • Emperor's New Groove (2000): Comedy movie about a greedy king and a humble villager who could use some friendship knowledge.
  • Euphoria (2019): An excellent diverse teenage drama about coming of age, and valuable lessons.
  • Parasite (2019): Academy Award for Best Picture, showcasing how social classes afffect relationships, MUST WATCH.
